SERVICES

Our service team has over forty years of experience.

When you bring your bike to The Mountain Spoke repair shop you can be certain that it is in good hands. Our service staff routinely attends product and service clinics so that they are always in the know about the latest in bicycle repair and maintenance. Our experienced, trained, and certified mechanics have all of the tools and skills to ensure that your ride runs as smoothly as the day you first brought it home.

48-Hour Turnaround Most services can be completed as you browse our store and take a little relaxing break. If the repair requires a bit more TLC, Mountain Spoke can guarantee that you’ll be back on your ride in 48 hours or less. If parts are needed to be ordered, we will complete the repair as soon as the parts arrive.

Please note, prices listed here are for estimation purposes only, and do not include the cost of parts that may be necessary. This is only a small sample of the repairs that we offer. Please bring your bike in and we will make an estimate with you to determine the work needed and cost. Prices are subject to change at anytime.

BIKE FITTINGS

WHAT SIZE BIKE DO YOU NEED?

We are more than happy to help you determine the best bike size for you, but you will need to come see us. Due to variations among types of bikes and manufacturers, as well as the amazingly diverse nature of the human body, there isn't a formula that magically takes your height or inseam and turns out the perfect bike size. We have a large variety of sizes and styles in stock, so we usually have several good options to show you, and you can test ride any bikes that you are interested in.

MONTHLY MAINTENANCE CLASS

WHAT YOU WILL LEARN

We offer service clinics to teach you how to keep your bike running smoothly, and to feel more confident and self-reliant when you're riding alone. This is a basic class that will teach flat repair and tire inflation, proper cleaning and lubrication and what you need to carry when you are out on the road or trail.
