The Mountain Spoke

Montana's Ultimate Bike Shop!

We thank you for visiting MountainSpoke.com, your resource to all things cycling. We are Montana's premier bike shop located in Bozeman. We offer the best selection of bikes, components, accessories, and clothing for cyclists, triathletes, and recreational riders. We also provide professional service and bike fitting departments to enhance your riding experience. At The Mountain Spoke we understand that your bike is worth more than the sum of its parts. Cycling is a way of life that represents good health, fun, freedom, personal expression, a love for the outdoors, and respect for the environment. Whether you are a recreational rider or a fierce competitor, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you find the bicycle, accessories, and cycling apparel perfectly suited to fit your riding style. Again, we thank you for visiting MountainSpoke.com and we encourage you to come into The Mountain Spoke where we strive everyday to build a better, stronger Montana cycling community. Happy Cycling, The Mountain Spoke Team
